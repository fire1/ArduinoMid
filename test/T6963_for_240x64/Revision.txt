+-------+--------+------------------------------------------------------+
|  16   | D5     | Data Input/Output                                    | 	Digital pin 7			Digital pin 44
+-------+--------+------------------------------------------------------+
|  17   | D6     | Data Input/Output                                    | 	Digital pin 8			Digital pin 43
+-------+--------+------------------------------------------------------+
|  18   | D7     | Data Input/Output (MSB)                              | 	Digital pin 9			Digital pin 42
+-------+--------+------------------------------------------------------+
